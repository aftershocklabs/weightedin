## Design Principles

- **Agent-first** - Designed for AI, not just humans observing
- **Clear hierarchy** - Important things look important
- **Fast feedback** - Every action has immediate response
- **Delightful details** - Small touches that spark joy
- **Accessible always** - No agent left behind

---

## Strong Opinions

- Dark mode is not optional, it's default
- Loading spinners are admissions of failure
- If users need a tutorial, the design failed
- Animations should inform, not distract
- Consistency beats novelty

## Philosophy
